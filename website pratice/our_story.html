Les Frères

OUR STORY

The bistro, called "Les Frères" (The Brothers) is located in vibrant Neubau district in Vienna. It is a cozy little spot with just a few tables, but it has a big reputation for serving delicious food made with the freshest local ingredients.

The brothers, Max and Laurent, had grown up on a farm in the Swiss countryside and had always been passionate about natural wine and farm-to-table cuisine.

They had spent years traveling the world and working in some of the finest restaurants, honing their skills and learning from the best.

The wine selection at Les Frères is very impressive. The brothers are obsessed with natural wine, which they source from small, independent winemakers who use organic and biodynamic methods.

Despite its small size, Les Frères quickly became a local favorite. Max and Laurent pour their hearts into every dish. And as they often joked, At Les Frères, it's not just about the food and the wine – it's about the brotherhood.
